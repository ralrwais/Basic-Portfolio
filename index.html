About Me

Hello, and welcome to my page. My name is Rima Alrwais and I am a young creative living and working in San Francisco. I am a very outgoing person, and there is rarely anything I wouldnt try once. One of my biggest passions is travel, I have visited 15 countries and counting.

Among other things like writing, singing, playing music and making short films, I develop web pages. This site is here to introduce you to my work and give you an idea of who I am as a person. Enjoy!
